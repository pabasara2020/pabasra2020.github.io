Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery





About the Hatchery

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is a well-known turtle conservation facility in Kosgoda, Sri Lanka. Its mission is to conserve and preserve sea turtles while also creating awareness about their significance and conservation requirements. The hatchery acts as a safe haven for sea turtle eggs and hatchlings, providing a secure environment for their development and ultimate release into the ocean.

Visitors at Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery may learn about numerous sea turtle species, their life cycles, and the obstacles they encounter in the wild. The hatchery provides educational events and guided tours for guests to learn about turtle conservation initiatives and hatchery operations. Staff and volunteers are knowledgeable about turtle nesting habits, nesting season, and the hatchery's efforts to safeguard these lovely creatures.
The ability to see newborn turtles hatch is one of the pleasures of a visit to the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery. Visitors may see the newborn hatchlings make their way to the water and experience the amazing moment when these little animals begin their voyage in the great sea.
The hatchery also plays an important role in nesting initiatives, where visitors may help locate and safeguard turtle nests. Depending on the hatchery's operations and guidelines, visitors may be able to participate in nesting or release programs, directly contributing to conservation efforts.
