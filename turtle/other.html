Other Turtle Hatcheries


Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is a well-known turtle conservation facility in Kosgoda, Sri Lanka. Its mission is to conserve and preserve sea turtles while also creating awareness about their significance and conservation requirements. The hatchery acts as a safe haven for sea turtle eggs and hatchlings, providing a secure environment for their development and ultimate release into the ocean.

Bentota Turtle Hatchery

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery is another renowned turtle conservation facility located in Bentota, Sri Lanka. This hatchery is dedicated to the conservation and protection of sea turtles, as well as educating the public about the importance of their preservation. It provides a nurturing environment for sea turtle eggs and hatchlings, ensuring their survival before they are released back into the ocean.
